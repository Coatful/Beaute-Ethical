Waterfull Green Sun Cream

Sun Protection

Product Features This moisturizing and brightening sun cream reduces the visibility of pores, wrinkles, and age spots while offering broad-spectrum UVA/UVB protection. It contains skin-soothing and antioxidant-rich ingredients that improve elasticity and restore the skin’s moisture balance without leaving a white cast.
这款保湿亮肤防晒霜可减少毛孔、皱纹和色斑的可见度，同时提供广谱UVA/UVB防护。富含舒缓和抗氧化成分，提升肌肤弹性并恢复水分平衡，不泛白。
Skin Type All skin types, including sensitive and irritated skin.
所有肤质，包括敏感和易刺激肌肤。
Main Ingredient • Niacinamide – Moisturizing, brightening, reduces pores, wrinkles, and age spots
  烟酰胺 — 保湿、美白，淡化毛孔、皱纹和色斑
• Tinosorb S – Broad-spectrum UVA protection, penetrates deep layers of the skin
  Tinosorb S — 广谱UVA防护，深入肌肤底层
• Uvinul T150 – UVB filter, provides stable and effective protection
  Uvinul T150 — 高效稳定的UVB防护
• Tinosorb M – High-level protection against UVA and UVB rays
  Tinosorb M — 高效UVA和UVB防护
