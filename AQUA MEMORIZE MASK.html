Aqua Memorize Mask

Aqua Memorize Line

Product Features With a shape memory system to help restore firmness in the skin, this moisturizing and firming mask contains betaine and panthenol for long-lasting moisturization while mannan and shea butter firm and moisturize the skin.
采用形状记忆系统，有助于恢复肌肤紧致度。这款保湿紧致面膜含有甜菜碱和泛醇，提供持久滋润，同时由甘露聚糖和乳木果脂紧致并滋养肌肤。
Skin Type All skin types, Dry and oily skin
所有肤质，干性与油性肌肤适用
Main Ingredient • Panthenol – Moisturizing, anti-acne (Vitamin B5)
  泛醇（维生素B5）— 保湿、抗痘
• Betaine – Moisturizing, gentle
  甜菜碱 — 温和保湿
• Biosaccharide gum – Moisturizing, soothing
  生物糖胶 — 保湿、舒缓
• Sodium hyaluronate – Moisture retention
  透明质酸钠 — 锁水保湿
• β-glucan – Powerful moisturizing agent
  β-葡聚糖 — 强效保湿成分
• Multiex BSASM – Soothing, anti-irritation
  Multiex BSASM — 舒缓抗刺激
• Mannan, shea butter – Firming and moisturizing
  甘露聚糖、乳木果脂 — 紧致与滋润
How to Use Apply all over the face. Leave for 15–20 minutes.
To use it as a sleeping pack: after cleansing in the evening, apply a suitable amount from the center to the outside of the face.
将面膜均匀涂抹于整个面部，敷15–20分钟后洗净。
也可作为晚间睡眠面膜使用：洁面后从面部中心向外涂抹适量。
Content 300ml
300毫升
